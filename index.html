About

Scrufster are a UK based group who make software for biological scientists. Our current projects are HabitApp and PrimerPipeline.

HabitApp

HabitApp provides a novel way of measuring the percentage cover provided by the tree canopy coverage in the field, turning your smart device or computer into a coverage measurement tool.

PrimerPipeline

PrimerPipeline is a user-friendly program enveloping an automated pipeline for microsatellite mining and primer design for de novo low coverage genomes.
